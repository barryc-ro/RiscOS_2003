LinkUp Systems doubles office space, moves to Santa Clara facility to accommodate company growth

Demand for LinkUp's Processor Plus Peripherals Solution Fuels Company Expansion

SANTA CLARA, CA - September 18, 2000: LinkUp Systems Corp., provider of single-chip ARM processor plus peripherals solutions for Internet and information appliances, today announced that the company has doubled its office space by moving to new quarters in Santa Clara, Calif.

LinkUp Systems provides innovative single-chip processor plus peripherals solutions to manufacturers of smart phones, Personal Digital Assistants, set-top boxes, MP3 players, bar code readers and other Internet and data appliances.

The company's L7200 family of single chip solutions combines an ARM720T processor with many highly-integrated system peripherals, such as memory and LCD controllers, Universal Serial Bus and Bluetooth wireless communications compatibility. LinkUp Systems also provides reference designs, development system support and an integrated debug environment for its products.

"The explosive growth of the Internet appliance and smart phone market has fueled the rapid expansion of LinkUp Systems," said Ravi Bhatnagar, President and CEO LinkUp Systems. "Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, our new high-tech facility will provide much-needed space for our growing number of employees and for the continued development of advanced products." LinkUp System's new address is 3401 Leonard Court, Santa Clara, CA 95054, near the 101 freeway's Montague Expressway exit. The phone number remains the same, (408) 988-1848 as does the fax number (408) 988-3848. The website address is www.linkupsys.com.

About LinkUp Systems

LinkUp Systems Corporation provides ARM CPU core-based, system-enabling solutions targeting the fast-growing Internet appliance and consumer electronics market. The company's L7200 processor family is optimized for low power portable applications (such as Pocket PCs, smart phones and Wireless Internet terminals), high performance desktop solutions such as screenphones, and mobile multimedia embedded markets. LinkUp also provides support chips for Intel's StrongARM® SA-1100 integrated processor. The company is headquartered at 3401 Leonard Court, Santa Clara, CA 95054 and can be reached at (408) 988-1848. More information is available at www.linkupsys.com.
