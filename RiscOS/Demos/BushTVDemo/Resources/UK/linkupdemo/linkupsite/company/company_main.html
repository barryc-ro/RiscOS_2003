Corporate Overview

LinkUp Systems' mission is to define, develop and distribute innovative "System-on-Silicon" solutions for the Internet appliance and consumer electronics market. LinkUp products and technology enable the system OEM to manufacture high performance systems while reducing design time and product cost. Located in Santa Clara, the company provides system solutions including IC, Development Systems, software drivers with OAL for major operating systems, integrated development environment, and software tools.

The company's products are targeted for the Palm-size PC, Internet enabled phone, personal communicator, smart phone, wireless internet terminals, electronic book, set-top box, and vertical embedded applications markets.

The company provides complete OEM system solutions, which consist of complete application reference platforms and manufacturing kits for targeted applications.

The Company also provides development systems, software tools, and integrated debug environment. The development system includes an OS port and software drivers for Windows CE and Symbian Epoc32.

LinkUp Systems' initial products include the L1110 and L1121 PC Card/CompactFlash Interface Chip, and the L7200 Internet System Processor

The company is well funded by venture funds and corporate funds (including Intel Corp. and Yamaha Corp.)
